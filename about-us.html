About Us

Omani Communications Limited was duly incorporated on the 11th of September 1999 with Registration Number : 365179, at the Corporate Affairs Commission in Nigeria.

We are a design and printing company. Since incorporation, Omani has been engaged in the provision of top quality printing and corporate branding services to discerning corporate bodies.

Our assets base currently is about 350 Million Naira.

Our proficiency in the area of offset printing is intrepid and thus with pride we believe in our pay off...Corporate printing Solutions.

Our corporate office/press is located at 1, Anjorin Lane, Off Olukole Street Surulere, Lagos.
